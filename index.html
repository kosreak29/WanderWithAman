03 — DESTINATIONS

Where to Next

From the sacred shores of Omkareshwar, where the gentle whispers of the Narmada carry centuries of devotion, to the spiritual energy of Salasar Balaji that echoes through the heart of Rajasthan, and onward to the tranquil emerald backwaters of Kerala—every destination is more than a place on the map; it is a chapter in a journey of discovery.


These upcoming adventures are not merely about covering distances, but about embracing stories, cultures, traditions, and the countless moments that make travel meaningful. Along the way, I hope to capture the soul of each destination through photographs, stories, and experiences that inspire curiosity and connection.


Join me as I continue exploring the diverse landscapes of India—one sunrise, one train ride, one conversation, and one unforgettable chapter at a time. The road ahead is long, the stories are waiting, and the journey has only just begun.

04 — About Aman

The Story Behind The Journey

Every destination carries a story waiting to be heard. What began as a simple desire to travel slowly evolved into a lifelong mission to document India through photography, words, and unforgettable experiences.

From spiritual cities like Ujjain to the coastlines of Vizag, every journey leaves behind a lesson, a memory, and a new perspective on life.
