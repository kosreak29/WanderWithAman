Salasar Balaji, Rajasthan

There are destinations known for their beauty.

And then there are destinations known for their devotion.

Salasar Balaji belongs to the second kind.

◆

For years I have heard stories of the unwavering faith that draws millions of devotees to this sacred place in Rajasthan. Every traveller who has visited speaks not only about the temple, but about the feeling they carried back home.

I often imagine beginning this journey across the vast landscapes of Rajasthan.

The endless highways. The warm desert winds. The changing colours of the sky.

And somewhere beyond those roads stands Salasar Balaji, welcoming everyone who arrives with hope in their hearts.

◆

I imagine walking through the temple complex surrounded by chants, prayers, and an energy that cannot be explained through photographs alone.

Because some experiences cannot be captured by a camera.

They can only be felt.

This journey is not about tourism. It is about surrender.

About leaving behind worries for a few moments and standing before something greater than ourselves.

◆

Perhaps the greatest gift of travel is not discovering new places.

Perhaps it is discovering new perspectives.

When the time comes, I hope this journey will remind me of that truth once again.

Until then, Salasar Balaji remains a chapter waiting to be written.
